About Us

Welcome to Best Cars dealership, home to the best cars in North America. We deal in sale of domestic and imported cars at reasonable prices.

CEO

Emily Johnson

As the CEO of Best Cars, Emily Johnson brings over 20 years of leadership experience in the automotive and retail sectors. She's passionate about building a customer-first culture and continuously improving every aspect of the dealership experience. Under her leadership, the dealership has expanded its inventory, enhanced digital platforms, and established itself as a trusted community leader in the automotive space. Emily's approach to leadership emphasizes innovation, transparency, and a focus on long-term relationships with both customers and employees. Outside of work, Emily is a mentor to young professionals and enjoys spending time hiking and exploring nature with her family.

emily.johnson@bestcars.com

CFO

Mark Williams

Mark Williams serves as the CFO of Best Cars, bringing more than 18 years of experience in financial management and strategic planning within the automotive industry. Mark oversees all financial operations, ensuring the dealership remains profitable while maintaining a strong commitment to reinvestment in both infrastructure and customer service. His expertise in financial analysis helps the company navigate market trends, control costs, and manage cash flow to drive sustainable growth. When he's not crunching numbers, Mark enjoys playing golf and is an advocate for financial literacy in his community.

mark.williams@bestcars.com
